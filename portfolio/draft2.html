~ ALL ABOUT CHRISTELLE! ~

Hi, my name is Christelle, and I am 17 years old. My birthday is November 12th, 2006. For those who like astrology, my sign is Scorpio. The water sign is seductive, passionate, and independent. Scorpios are known for their passionate and determined nature. They are often described as mysterious and intuitive individuals. I was born in Haiti and traveled a lot when I was younger, which is one of the things I love to do to this day. I have always been fascinated by different cultures and experiencing new places. Exploring new destinations and immersing myself in diverse traditions has broadened my perspective on the world. I am a high school senior and a college freshman due to the college classes that I take. I have always been passionate about learning and challenging myself academically. Taking college classes while still in high school has allowed me to explore subjects that truly interest me and prepare for the next chapter of my education. In the near future, I will be studying to become a sonograper and a NICU nurse. My love for babies has helped me figure out what I want to do with my career. I am fascinated by the intricate process of fetal development and the opportunity to provide care for newborns in critical conditions. Pursuing a career as a sonographer and NICU nurse will allow me to combine my passion for healthcare with my love for infants, ensuring that I make a meaningful impact in their lives from the very beginning. Interesting, right? Well, I'm not done. Follow me so I can tell you more about myself.

More about Scorpios

October 23 - November 21

Originating from the constellation Scorpius, Scorpio is the eighth sign of the zodiac. Its range of ecliptic longitude is 210–240°. The Sun passes through this sign under the tropical zodiac, on average, from October 23 to November 21. A person born under the influence of Scorpio may be referred to as a Scorpio or a Scorpionic, depending on the zodiac system they adopt.
